## What is Order Undo?

**Order Undo** is a conversion-recovery tool that adds a customizable "Undo" period directly to the Shopify Order Status page. It allows customers to:
*   **Cancel orders instantly** without waiting for support.
*   **Edit order details** to fix simple mistakes like addresses or quantities.
*   **Reduce support tickets** for merchants, allowing teams to focus on growth instead of cancellations.
